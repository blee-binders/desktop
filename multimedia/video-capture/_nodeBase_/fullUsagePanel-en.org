** +
** VCR Player: Samsung VCR-DVD V2000 --- Any VCR Player will do
** AVR (Audio Video Receiver): Denon AVR-2808CI -- https://www.crutchfield.com/S-1tm8u4q0cm7/p_033AV2808C/Denon-AVR-2808CI.html
** AVR Remote Control: Denon RC-1068 --- irrelevant for this.
** Any AVR which can output HDMI will Do.
** -----
** Irrelevant Setup:
**   -- DVD output to HDP Input
**   -- AVR CD Audio Input -- PC Main output
**   -- AVR DVR Audio Input -- PC Blue Yeti Microphone Speaker Jack
** Relevant Setup:
**   -- VCR Audio And Video Output is going to AVR's-DVD-Input
**   -- VCR Audio Output is also going to USB 2 Adapter
**   -- AVR HDMI Output -- Goes to  Flint 4KP
